About me

I am from Buenos Aires City, Argentina, where I grew up, observed and studied Audiovisual Design, motivated by the idea of telling stories and conflicts through the power of the gaze, the camera and the poetics of the abstract. Today I do it between Barcelona and Berlin, where I work as filmmaker, editor, screenwriter and photographer. I exhibit and develop photographic, narrative and performance projects in which I portray the suspended geometry of light and space. I focus my poetry on social inequality and the contemplation of time through the body. I've been narrative montage teacher at the University of Buenos Aires for seven years and an editor of series, documentaries and short films. I grew up and continue working within the advertising world, alongside great and talented creative teams.
